We are proud to announce the launch of our new Litestar website! We've created a modern, comprehensive platform that serves as the central hub for everything regarding Litestar and it's ecosystem.

## What's New

Our redesigned website brings together all the resources you need to build amazing applications with Litestar, featuring a clean design, improved navigation, and new features.

### üîå Plugin Directory

One of our most exciting new features is the **Plugin Directory** - a comprehensive catalog of official and community plugins that extend Litestar's capabilities. Browse by category, search for specific functionality, and discover tools that can accelerate your development workflow.

**Featured plugin categories:**

- **Database**: Advanced Alchemy, SQLSpec, and more
- **Authentication**: JWT, OAuth integrations
- **Development Tools**: Testing utilities, development servers
- **Frontend Integration**: Vite, HTMX support

#### Submit Your Plugin

Have you created a plugin for Litestar? We'd love to feature it in our directory! To submit your plugin:

1. Fork the [litestar-org/plugins](https://github.com/litestar-org/plugins) repository
2. Add your plugin information following our submission guidelines
3. Create a pull request with your plugin details

Our team will review your submission and help showcase your contribution to the Litestar ecosystem.
